Obtaining a linkcode

  1. Click on the "Open Zermelo" button to open zermelo, and login when asked. If you are a teacher, choose webapp. You should see a screen like this:
  2. In the zermelo portal, click button with the settings icon in the sidebar
  3. In the "settings" menu, click on the button with the phone link icon as marked in the top image. This will open a popup modal with a QR code, your Zermelo instance id and your generated linkcode, like the bottom image. You can ignore the QR code. The above red-boxed text is your instance id, the lower text your linkcode. Sadly those values can not be copied so you have to type them over by yourself.
  4. Close this dialog and enter the linkcode in the assigned field.
